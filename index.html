The Epitome of the American Dream

Howard Hughes will forever be remembered as an true representation of the American dream. Mr. Hughes also represents how some dreams can be crushed under the weight of themselves. Howard Hughes lived a life that was extravagant in each and every manner, from innovative aviation advancements, to setting records in the height of America's fascination with aviation.

The Legacy of Howard Hughes

Howard Hughes broke many speed and distance records along with many other inventions and innovations that revolutionized American aviation. "Mr. Hughes" as people called him, was at one point more popular than the renowned Charles Lindbergh.
